Game Modes on MythicMC

Simplicity meets excitement. Dive into our curated Minecraft experiences: classic Survival, thrilling Duels, the unique challenge of OneBlock, and fast-paced KitPvP — all hacker-free and community-focused.

Survival

Build, explore, and thrive in classic Survival mode. Experience Minecraft the way it was meant to be played — fair, fun, and full of adventure.

Duels

Show off your PvP prowess in fast-paced one-on-one battles. Clean gameplay, no hackers, just pure skill and competition.

OneBlock

Start small on a single block and expand your world with resourceful mining and crafting. A fresh spin on skyblock challenges with MythicMC’s quality.

KitPvP

Choose your kit and jump straight into fast, action-packed PvP battles. Perfect your skills and dominate the arena in a fair environment.
